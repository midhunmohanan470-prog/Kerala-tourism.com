Popular Destinations

Munnar

Famous for tea plantations, rolling hills, and misty landscapes – perfect for honeymooners and nature lovers.

Alleppey

The Venice of the East – known for houseboat cruises, backwaters, and beautiful lagoons.

Varkala Beach

Golden sands, cliffs, and stunning sunsets – one of Kerala's most scenic beaches.
