Discover Alleppey

Alleppey, known as the Venice of the East, is famous for its backwaters, houseboat cruises, and beautiful lagoons. It is one of the most picturesque destinations in Kerala.

Top Experiences

Houseboat Cruises

Sail through serene backwaters in traditional houseboats and experience the true essence of Alleppey.

Backwaters

Explore the endless network of canals, lagoons, and lakes that make Alleppey unique.

Alleppey Beach

Relax on golden sands, watch beautiful sunsets, and visit the historic pier stretching into the sea.

Travel Tips

  • Best time to visit: November to February.
  • Don’t miss a houseboat stay on the backwaters.
  • Try local seafood and traditional Kerala meals.
