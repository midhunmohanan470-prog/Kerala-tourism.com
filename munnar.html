About Munnar

Munnar is a beautiful hill station in Kerala, famous for its lush tea plantations, misty hills, waterfalls, and wildlife. Situated at 1,600 meters above sea level, Munnar is one of the most popular honeymoon and family destinations in South India.

Things to Do in Munnar

Visit Tea Plantations

Explore sprawling tea estates and taste fresh tea at the Tata Tea Museum.

Eravikulam National Park

Home to the endangered Nilgiri Tahr and the beautiful Neelakurinji flowers.

Visit Waterfalls

Enjoy the scenic beauty of Attukal, Lakkam, and Nyayamakad waterfalls.
